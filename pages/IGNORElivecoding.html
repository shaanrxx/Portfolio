Live Coding

Explore a selection of my favourite live coding performances from the past year, including live sets, collaborations, and experiments in algorithmic music and creative coding.

Through live coding, I create music in real time by writing and manipulating code using Strudel, generating evolving rhythms, melodies, and sonic textures. I treat code as both a compositional tool and a performance instrument, blending programming, improvisation, and electronic music into dynamic audiovisual experiences.

I perform under the name shapesandjapes, exploring the creative possibilities of computation and sound while embracing experimentation, spontaneity, and play.
