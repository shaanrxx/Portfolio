About Project

The website: ‘DIGITAL DIALOGUE: Unveiling the AI Chatbot Experience’ is a platform for exploring and understanding the integration of AI chatbots into our daily lives.

This site consists of six articles, each grappling with different themes surrounding AI: interactive storytelling, romantic AI relationships, mental health, and the future of chatbots.
