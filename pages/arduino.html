Arduino: Art Installation

About the Project

The project evolved from creating a tactile art installation for children, aiming to teach colors and sounds through interaction, to a sophisticated art piece integrating sound and light. Initially planned as an audio-only experience, inspiration from social media and online tutorials led to the incorporation of visual elements using conductive paint and LED lights. Techniques from various projects were adapted, including using conductive paint for piano-like interaction and programming LEDs for visual feedback. The design, executed on plywood with a laser cutter, combined interactive elements with artistic aesthetics, culminating in an immersive experience for viewers.
